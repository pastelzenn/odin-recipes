Pannang Curry

Mouthwatering Spicy Curry With Chicken pictured above

  1. Saute Vegetables: Heat oil over medium high heat in large non stick skillet.
  2. Add Curry Paste and Peanut Butter and Saute for 1 minute. Then add 1/2 cans of cocnut milk
  3. Add Chicken Simmer for 10-15 minutes (or until chicken is no longer pink.
  4. Final Touches Stir in sugar, fish sauce, lime juice, and simmer for 5 minutes. Season wth Salt & Pepper for taste.

Ingredients Needed!

Chicken: You can substitute the chicken for Shrimp or Tofu

Rice: Brown, white, or jasmine rice pair well with curry, or skip the rice! Noodles are fine too.

Curry Paste: You can do many things with this. For simplest form-factor just buy store-bought curry paste!

Vegetables: Broccoli, carrots, and peppers are amazing for this dish!
