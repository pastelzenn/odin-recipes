Murgh Kari

Beautifully spicy Murgh Kari from India with chicken chunks

  1. Step One: Gather ingredients and sprinkle chicken breats with 2 teaspoons salt. Heat oil in large skiller over high heat; partically cook the chicken in the hot oil in batches until completely browned on all sides, then transfer to plate.
  2. Step Two: Reduce heat to medium and add onion, garlic, and ginger to the oil remaining in the skillet. Cook and Stir until onion turns soft and translucent, 5 to 8 minutes. Stir curry powder, cumin, tumeric, coriander, cayenne, and 1 tablespoon of water into mixture.
  3. Step Three: Add tomatoes, yogurt, 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro, and 1 teaspoon salt to the mixture, stir to combine.
  4. Step Four: Return chicken breast to the skillet along with any juices on the plate. Pour in 1/2 cup water and bring to a boil. Turn the chicken to coat with the sauce, sprinkle pepper and cilantro over the chicken
  5. Step 5: Cover the skillet and simmer until breasts are no longer pink in the center and juices run clear, around 20 minutes. Serve after thermometer inserted into center reads 165 degrees.

Ingredients Needed!

Chicken, onion, garlic, ginger, tomatoes, curry powder (of your choosing) coconut milk, and various spices are usually used in creating this dish! Don't forget you can always mix up the recipe and include your own ingredients.
